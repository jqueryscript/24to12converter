We do not collect any personal information from you automatically. Our website is a simple tool and does not require user accounts, logins, or any form of registration. The time data you enter for conversion is processed in your browser and is not stored on our servers.

Cookies

We do not use cookies to collect personal information. We may use cookies for the sole purpose of website functionality and analytics, such as understanding user preferences. You can instruct your browser to refuse all cookies or to indicate when a cookie is being sent.
