Printable Military Time Chart

Updated June 13, 2026

Military time uses the 24-hour clock. This printable chart shows the standard AM/PM equivalent for each full hour of the day.

Direct answer

To read military time, use the first two digits as the hour and the last two digits as the minutes. For example, 1730 military time means 5:30 PM.

How to use the chart

For times after 1259, subtract 1200 from the military time to get the PM hour in 12-hour time. For example, 1830 minus 1200 gives 630, so 1830 is 6:30 PM.

For times from 0000 through 1159, use AM. The exception is 0000, which is 12:00 AM rather than 0:00 AM.
